# Market-Ready Notes

## Recommended positioning

Use this wording:

> EnergyWise Homes Australia is a web-based home-energy check-up that helps households identify common energy-wasting features and choose practical actions to reduce bills. It provides indicative savings and a personalised action plan based on simple household inputs.

Avoid this wording:

> This app guarantees bill savings.
> This app is a certified home energy audit.
> This app replaces professional assessment.
> This app calculates exact savings.

## Target audience

- Australian households
- Renters
- Owner-occupiers
- Councils and community energy programmes
- Sustainability educators
- Behaviour-change and energy-advice projects

## Strongest value proposition

Most public-facing energy tools become too technical too quickly. This app works because it starts with common household decisions: thermostat settings, shower duration, LED lighting, standby power, curtains, draughts, and insulation awareness.

## Weak points to fix before serious dissemination

1. Savings model is still simplified.
2. No climate-zone logic yet.
3. No energy retailer tariff integration.
4. No appliance-specific audit.
5. No validation study with households.
6. No formal user-testing results.

These are not fatal weaknesses for a prototype, but they are fatal if the app is marketed as an exact calculator.
